Luigi’s Legal Fund Bookshop FAQ

What is Luigi’s Legal Fund Bookshop?

Luigi’s Legal Fund Bookshop is an affiliate bookstore on Bookshop.org. Each purchase generates a 10% commission that is fully donated to Luigi’s legal defense fund. It complements direct donations while letting supporters contribute by purchasing books they love.

Why does it exist?

The shop was created as a transparent way to support Luigi’s legal expenses through everyday purchases, ensuring all commissions benefit his fund.

Why use Bookshop.org?

Bookshop.org supports independent bookstores and distributes revenue fairly, unlike major retailers. This supports local economies while resisting monopolies.

Purchasing & Payment

Currently, purchases require a US-based payment method and shipping address. Only physical books are available—ebooks and audiobooks are not supported at this time.

Finances & Transparency

Luigi has no involvement in the shop due to federal regulations. 100% of affiliate commissions go directly to his legal fund. View our latest financial reports.

How are book purchases split?

Each sale benefits Bookshop.org, independent bookstores, publishers, and authors. This shop earns 10% per sale—all of which is donated to Luigi’s fund. See affiliate policies.

Does Luigi pay taxes on these funds?

The shop owner receives a 1099 form and is responsible for taxes. Any tax implications for Luigi depend on his personal filings. This is not tax advice.

Is it safe to purchase here?

Yes. Bookshop.org securely processes all transactions. We provide full transparency through published financial reports.

How do I know this isn’t a scam?

We maintain transparency with published earnings reports. Bookshop.org processes payments, adding a layer of trust.

Privacy & Data Security

Bookshop.org handles all personal data securely. We do not collect or share personal information beyond what is necessary to process orders.
