Luigi’s Bookshop FAQ and Info

The Bookshop is an affiliate storefront on Bookshop.org. We earn a 10% affiliate commission on purchases made through our links. That commission is paid to the shop founder’s bank account, then fully transferred to the official GiveSendGo legal defense fund.

Additionally, Bookshop.org shares part of its profits with independent bookstores via their profit pool. Learn more here.

Does my purchase have to be on your book lists? No. As long as you use the affiliate link and see "Luigi's Legal Fund Bookshop" at the top of your Bookshop.org screen, your purchase counts toward the 10% commission.

Can the shop founder see who is buying? No. We can see what items are purchased, but never who buys them. All fulfillment is handled by Bookshop.org.

All affiliate commissions are publicly tracked and transferred to Luigi’s official fund. For financial transparency, view our latest financial reports here: View Financial Reports.

Why isn't Luigi involved in the shop or its finances? Because of prison rules, Luigi cannot legally participate in or benefit directly from any business venture. If you think that’s unjust, consider contacting your representatives and supporting efforts to end the death penalty.

For issues with your order, refunds, or shipping, contact Bookshop.org directly: Request a Refund or Report a Problem.

For questions about this shop or fund, please scroll to the bottom of our Linktree to use the Contact Us form.
