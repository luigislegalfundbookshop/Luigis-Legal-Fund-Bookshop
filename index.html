Luigi’s Bookshop FAQ and Info

About Luigi’s Legal Fund Bookshop

What is Luigi’s Legal Fund Bookshop?

Luigi’s Legal Fund Bookshop is an affiliate bookstore operated through Bookshop.org. Each purchase made via this shop generates a 10% commission that is fully directed to support Luigi’s legal defense fund.

Why does it exist?

It's a transparent, trustworthy way to financially support Luigi’s legal fund through book purchases, in addition to direct donations.

Why use Bookshop.org?

Bookshop.org supports independent bookstores rather than major online retailers, sustaining community-based book culture and promoting fairer income distribution.

Purchasing & Payment

Can I purchase from the bookshop if I’m outside the United States?

Currently, purchases require a U.S.-based payment method and shipping address. Bookshop.org does not yet support international payments or shipping.

Why aren’t ebooks or audiobooks available?

Bookshop.org affiliate shops only offer physical books at this time. If this changes, we’ll update the shop information.

Finances & Transparency

Why isn’t Luigi involved in the shop or its finances?

Federal regulations prohibit incarcerated individuals from receiving funds or conducting business. For example:

“Funds enclosed in inmate correspondence are to be rejected except as provided for in part 506 of this chapter.” — 28 C.F.R. § 540.23

Therefore, Luigi is not involved in any operations or funds management for the shop.

If you think this is a silly rule, contact your representatives about it and help end the death penalty here: End the Death Penalty

How is the money split?

Each book purchase divides revenue among:

  • Bookshop.org for platform and fulfillment costs
  • Independent bookstores
  • 10% commission to Luigi’s legal fund

Does Luigi pay taxes on this income?

The shop owner receives a Form 1099 and is personally responsible for any taxes. Luigi is responsible for determining any tax liabilities on the legal fund’s end. This is not tax advice.

Is this shop safe and legit?

All purchases are processed by Bookshop.org, a secure and reputable platform. Full transparency is maintained through published financial reports.
